Dementia Care

Floreat World Care Services offers comprehensive Dementia Care services to support individuals with dementia and their families. Our team of experienced and compassionate caregivers are specially trained in providing personalized care for individuals with varying stages of dementia.


We understand that each individual's needs are unique, and so we develop personalized care plans that cater to their specific requirements. Our services include assistance with activities of daily living, medication management, transportation to appointments, and engagement in meaningful activities that promote cognitive stimulation and socialization.


Our caregivers are also trained to manage challenging behaviors that are commonly associated with dementia. We work closely with families and healthcare professionals to ensure that the individual's needs are met, and their quality of life is maintained. At Floreat World Care Services, we are dedicated to maintaining a safe and comfortable environment for individuals with dementia. We provide compassionate and respectful care that honors the individual's dignity and promotes their overall well-being.

Our Services Include

  • Personalised dementia care at all stages
  • Clinical expertise within dementia care
  • The ability to work in conjunction with other medical professionals
  • Knowledge of memory training activities to reduce symptoms
  • Advice on how family can support
  • Medication management (if required)
  • Dementia friendly activities

FAQs

Q1
What is Dementia Care?

Dementia Care is specialized care for individuals who are living with dementia. It focuses on providing personalized care and support to individuals and their families as they navigate the challenges associated with this condition.

Q2
Who provides Dementia Care?

Dementia Care is provided by a team of healthcare professionals that includes trained caregivers, nurses, social workers, and other specialists who work together to provide holistic care to individuals and their families.

Q3
What types of services are provided in Dementia Care?

Dementia Care services include assistance with activities of daily living, medication management, transportation to appointments, engagement in meaningful activities that promote cognitive stimulation and socialization, and management of challenging behaviors that are common among individuals with dementia.

Q4
Is Dementia Care only for individuals in advanced stages of dementia?

No, Dementia Care can be provided to individuals in varying stages of dementia. It is intended to support individuals and their families as they navigate the challenges associated with this condition and improve their quality of life.

Q5
Is Dementia Care covered by insurance?

Dementia Care may be covered by some insurance plans, including Medicaid and long-term care insurance. It is important to check with your insurance provider to determine what services are covered.

Q6
How can I access Dementia Care services?

You can access Dementia Care services through your healthcare provider, hospital, or by contacting a Dementia Care provider directly. It is important to work with a provider who has experience and training in providing care for individuals with dementia.
